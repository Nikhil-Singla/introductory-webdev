Ash Ketchum (Japanese: サトシ Satoshi) is the main character of my favourite cartoon, Pokémon. He was also featured in various manga based on the anime, such as "The Electric Tale of Pikachu", "Ash & Pikachu", and "Pocket Monsters Diamond & Pearl".

He's a Pokémon Trainer hailing from Pallet Town, wanting to be a Master.
His starter was a Pikachu, though I hoped he could've picked Charmander instead.
On his journeys around the world, he's been accompanied by a multitude of close friends.
In Sun & Moon, he finally becomes a Champion of the Alola region, which fulfills his longtime dreams.
In Pokémon Journeys, he becomes one of the eight best Trainers in the world within the World Coronation Series.
He shares his Japanese name with the creator of the Pokémon franchise, Satoshi Tajiri. His English surname is a pun of the English motto, "Gotta catch 'em all!. [CLICK ME!]"
